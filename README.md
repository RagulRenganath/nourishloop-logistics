NourishLoop is a high-fidelity, single-page application (SPA) designed to solve the logistical and economic friction in corporate food redistribution. 

Developed as a comprehensive sustainability project, this platform bridges the gap between premium corporate donors, verified NGOs, and volunteer logistics fleets. It translates sustainability into tangible business metrics through automated ESG reporting, liability management, and algorithmic routing.

## 💼 Core Enterprise Architecture

The platform operates across three interconnected portals, utilizing a simulated LocalStorage database for persistent state management across sessions.

### 1. Corporate Donor Portal (Hotels & Banquets)
Designed to incentivize corporate participation by turning waste reduction into measurable financial and operational ROI.
* **ESG Financial Dashboard:** Live calculations of tax offset value (₹) and methane diversion (kg).
* **AI Predictive Analytics:** Structural waste modeling to advise kitchen prep reduction (powered by Chart.js).
* **Smart Contracts Ledger:** Immutable tracking of digital liability waivers to shield donor brands.
* **Anonymous Geofence Dispatch:** Allows luxury brands to broadcast surplus to local NGOs without risking brand image.

### 2. Verified NGO Portal (Receivers)
Designed to prevent secondary waste by ensuring shelters only receive what they can safely handle.
* **Live Radar:** Scans a 5km geofence for active corporate surplus.
* **Capacity Management:** Dynamic limits to prevent claiming food beyond available refrigeration space.
* **Dietary Engine:** Strict filters to ensure claimed food matches the cultural and health needs of the shelter.

### 3. Fleet Logistics Portal (Volunteers)
Designed to optimize routing and gamify the volunteer experience to prevent burnout.
* **Active Telemetry:** Live, interactive routing maps powered by Leaflet.js.
* **Clustered Routing:** Algorithmic batching of nearby pickups to reduce fuel consumption.
* **Corporate Rewards:** A gamified "Karma" system redeemable for fuel vouchers and coffee sponsorships.
